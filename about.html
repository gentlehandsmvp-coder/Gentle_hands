About Gentle Hands Zambia

Compassionate, professional in-home care delivered with dignity and trust

🎯 Our Mission

To provide compassionate, professional in-home care by connecting verified healthcare professionals with families in need, ensuring dignity, quality, and peace of mind.

📖 Our Story

Gentle Hands Zambia was founded by two healthcare professionals who saw the growing need for reliable, compassionate in-home care. Driven by a shared vision of bringing quality healthcare into the home, they built a platform that connects families with verified nurses who deliver care with kindness and professionalism.
